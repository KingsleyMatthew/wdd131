Mercedes-Benz & Luxury Cars

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Mercedes Luxury Hub

The name "Mercedes Luxury Hub" represents a website focused on showcasing Mercedes-Benz and other luxury cars. It highlights elegance, performance, and modern automotive design, making it suitable for car enthusiasts and potential buyers.

Optional Domain: mercedesluxuryhub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide users with information about Mercedes-Benz and luxury cars, including car specifications, comparisons, and image galleries. The site will also include interactive features such as filtering cars by type and viewing detailed information using JavaScript.

1. Target Audience & Scenarios

Target Audience

Car enthusiasts, luxury car buyers, and users interested in Mercedes-Benz models.

Scenario 1

A user wants to compare different Mercedes-Benz models before buying.

  • What are the differences between C-Class, E-Class, and G-Class?
  • Which model has the best performance and price?

Scenario 2

A user wants to explore luxury cars visually.

  • What do the latest Mercedes-Benz cars look like?
  • Can I view images of different models?

Nigeria

Nigeria, Africa's most populous nation, has a rich history originating from ancient civilizations like the Nok (c. 500 BC) and powerful pre-colonial states (Hausa Kingdoms, Oyo Empire, Benin Kingdom). Colonized by Britain by 1914, it gained independence on October 1, 1960. Following a civil war (1967-70) and years of military rule, it established a stable democracy in 1999.
